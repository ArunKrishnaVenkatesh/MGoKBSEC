About MGoKBSEC

Student Entrepreneurship Cell of Manipal Government of Karnataka BioIncubator

Our Story

MGoKBSEC was established to foster an entrepreneurial culture among students at Manipal University, in partnership with the Karnataka BioIncubator. We provide a platform for aspiring student entrepreneurs to transform their innovative ideas into successful ventures.

Since our inception, we have supported over 50 student startups across diverse sectors including biotechnology, healthcare, agriculture, and education. Our mission is to create a thriving ecosystem where students can learn, experiment, and launch their entrepreneurial journeys with confidence.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to launch and grow successful startups, particularly in the biotechnology and life sciences sectors.

👁️

Our Vision

To be Karnataka's leading student entrepreneurship cell, creating a pipeline of innovative startups that contribute to the state's bioeconomy and solve real-world challenges.
